Quality Education Book Cover

Science-Fiction author, Gray Rinehart, commissioned a new cover for the revised twentieth anniversary edition of his book, Quality Education.

Full bleed cover for Quality Education.

Key Project Skills/Components

  • Graphic Design
  • Visual Development
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe InDesign

I presented designs that explored the author's suggestions along with ideas based on my own interpretations from excerpts of the text and conversational anecdotes. Since the book was going to be present online as well as on store shelves, the final design uses bold text and strong color to catch the eye while quickly scrolling online, while subtle blueprints tell a deeper story and reward exploration for printed copies. The book debuted in the Top 10 on Amazon's Education book list.
