PSISTORM Gaming Jersey

The Starcraft 2 team, PSISTORM, needed a team jersey for their appearance at Blizzcon 2016.

Mockup of selected jersey.

Designing the jersey for PSISTORM was one of the highlights of my time at REACTIV. I met with the team founder and owner, along with the team manager, to get a better understanding of the organization's culture. We went through several rounds of critiques before settling on a design backed by both the players and management.
