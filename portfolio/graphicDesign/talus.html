Talus Software Logo

Talus Software, a technology service company that improves automotive safety through applied mathematics, needed a logo for their appearance at an international symposium for autonomous vehicles.

The final design presents a stylized "talus" emphasized through the use of line weight and shape in the negative spaces that depict the shape of a seatbelt.

I worked with the owner and founder of Talus Software to get an understanding of what the owner wanted to communicate through the logo. I played with the literal geographic definition of talus—a sloping mass of rock fragments at the foot of a cliff—combined with iconography for automotive safety features. Several iterations were created over a few rounds of critiques to arrive at the final design.
