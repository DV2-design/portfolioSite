REACTIV

REACTIV is an apparel start up created to bring new performance and recovery technology to the world of eSports.

The logo (top) and logotype (bottom) for REACTIV.

Key Project Skills/Components

  • Graphic Design
  • Visual Development
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe InDesign

I helped to establish REACTIV in the middle of 2014. I led the development of the values and goals of the brand, which informed the design of the logotype and the organization's visual language. The well defined edges and bold color usage on bright backgrounds built a recognizable image.

A selection of slides from an investor marketing slide deck.

As the Creative Director, I led a small design team of 5 people in the creation of customer facing materials such as marketing presentations, trade show print collateral, social media ads, promotional videos, product brochures, and team jerseys.

I delegated tasks and made budget-conscious decisions in order to deliver projects on time and with high fidelity.
