Cary Oil Dashboards

Cary Oil, a prominent gas station supplier in the Southeast, wanted to update their internal reporting system.

The new dashboard uses ample whitespace to keep components distinct and standardized widget sizes to aid in navigation, decreasing visual fatigue.

Key Project Skills/Components

  • UI/UX
  • CSS
  • Sketch
  • Sencha

When I was asked to update the internal site UI for Cary Oil, I saw an opportunity to address some quality of life changes at the same time. I shadowed employees and noted how they navigated through the pages of the site, and interviewed them on changes they would want to see made to help them work better.

While livening up the visual styles, I suggested changes to the information architecture that maintained quick navigation while making page content and tables easier to parse. Some of the visual language precedents were brought over to a parallel customer facing product.

Based on the previous internal dashboard, this customer facing tool focuses on quick status updates and recent activity.

Slightly exaggerated weights are used to increase clarity at a glance and aid in quick navigation.
