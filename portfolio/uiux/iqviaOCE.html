IQVIA OCE

IQVIA (formerly Quintiles IMS), a leading biopharmaceutical consultant and developer, had a goal to unite their international sales teams by merging their platforms to create a central sales hub.

Example pages from the application's sales portal.

Key Project Skills/Components

  • UI/UX
  • Graphic Design
  • Sketch
  • React.js
  • Agile development
  • SalesForce / Lightning
  • JIRA/Confluence

The Orchestrated Customer Engagement (OCE) by IQVIA was a synthesis of three products into a single platform. As the features were being built out their new proprietary design system, Apollo, was being developed.

As a principle visual designer I collaborated with the product owners to map out the workflows for key features and solidify user stories.
