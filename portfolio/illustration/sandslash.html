Sandslash in Blue + Gold

A gilded take on Whistler's "Harmony in Blue + Gold."

Finished piece measuring 16" x 20".

This piece was an experiment in material exploration. I looked to Whistler's "Peacock Room" combined with contemporary pop culture subject matter for inspiration.

I digitally drafted several compositions that mirrored Whistler's original, but ultimately decided to make the piece have a solitary atmosphere to it through a minimal layout. I transferred the pencil sketch to the painted surface with white chalk before applying gold leaf to the finished piece.

Key Project Skills/Components

  • Illustration
  • Painting
