Lunch comic panels

What started out as doodles to let coworkers know I was out to lunch grew into a series of single panel comics, exploring a range of ideas about life. They were digitally sketched and refined, then published to various social media platforms. The series contains 31 panels in total.

Most panels feature the character Juice, a young turtle with an unusually large head, as he explores the world around him.
