Fate Foretold Card Illustrations

I produced a set of illustrations for Fate Foretold, a tarot-inspired fantasy card game.

Final card illustrations with their official card borders. (left) The Unseen Road, (center) Calamity, (right) The Throne.

I was commissioned to produce art for three cards: The Unseen Road, Calamity, The Throne. I worked from artist briefs to produce concept sketches and after a round of critiques I refined these into illustrations to be used for the playing cards as well as promotional posters.

Key Project Skills/Components

  • Illustration
  • Digital Painting
