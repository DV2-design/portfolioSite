Forest Baron

A fantasy twist on John Singer Sargent's "Antonio Mancini."

Final digital render of the piece.

As an exercise, I wanted to create a derivative piece based on a master work—in this case, John Singer Sargent's "Portrait of Antonio Mancini".

After I analyzed the source material and completed several anatomy studies, I altered the color scheme and replaced Sargent's original subject with an owl.
